About Our Roots

Long ago, a wandering samurai from Japan made it to the USA, and settled down in the country. This man would eventually become the founder of this cafe, and his name was Kaedehara Kazuha. It used to be a small pub that attracted regulars rather than many newcomers. Despite its small amount of visitors, the cafe thrived.

One day, Kazuha found out about coffee, and he started selling that instead of beers. However, it seems that his coffee was blessed, for it was much more phenomenal than all other coffees served in the entire world. The store attracted more newcomers, and branches started to get established around the world.
