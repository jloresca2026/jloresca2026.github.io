Pierro

He is the First Harbinger. Nothing is publicly known about him, but it is said that he comes from a fallen nation called Khaen'riah.

Il Dottore

The Doctor is an exiled scholar of the Sumeru Akademiya. Due to his outlandish ideals, the people feared him. Eventually, he was taken in by the Fatui Harbingers. He is well-known for his morally questionable scientific experiements and his clones. He is known as the Second Harbinger.

Columbina

She is the Third Harbinger. Unfortunately, information about her cannot be disclosed.

Capitano

Capitano is the Fourth Harbinger, also known as one of the strongest humans in the continent. He is well-known for his formidable combat skills.

Pulcinella

Being the Fifth Harbinger, he seems to hold political importance in the nation of Snezhnaya.

Scaramouche

He is the Sixth Harbinger. An abandoned vessel of a God, Scaramouche holds a lot of power. It is truly such a shame he left the harbingers.

Arlecchino

Arlecchino is the Seventh Harbinger. She runs the House of Hearth, which is an orphanage that raises kids with the Fatui's ideals.

Signora

Although deceased, her legacy as the Eighth Harbinger continues to live on. She is an intelligent individual, having studied in the Sumeru Akademiya. She is also known as the Crimson Witch of Flames.

Pantalone

Pantalone is the Ninth Harbinger. He holds economic power, being one of the wealthiest in the entire continent. His goal is to make Snezhnaya "the heart that pumps money around the world". He also handles the Northland Bank.

Sandrone

Little can be publicly disclosed by the Tenth Harbinger. However, she occasionally researches on automatons and holds a great interest in puppets.

Tartaglia

Being the Eleventh Harbinger, he is known to be both the youngest and the weakest at the same time. Do not be fooled by this however, for his bloodlthirstiness is not something to be messed with.
